The Path Onward

Slinky Doge began its journey as an exceptionally ambitious token, characterized by perhaps one of the fairest distributions achievable through its innovative 'Fairdrop' mechanism, and subsequent initial liquidity offering at a market cap of $30. Here, we delineate the project's future trajectory. (Click each bulletpoint to expand for further details.)

STEP 1.

Slinky is born!
Initial Launch of Slinky Doge
Slinky Doge launched on Feb-11-2024 05:34:28 AM +UTC with a fairdrop distribution model. It allocated 100 tokens evenly among 50 wallets, reserving the rest for liquidity and marketing. Starting with a modest $60 market cap, Slinky Doge aims to find it's fair market valuation through gradual repositioning and rebasing.

Introducing Slinky Swap: Empowering Decentralized Token Listings and Slinky Liquidity Growth
Slinky Swap introduces an innovative decentralized listing process designed for token projects. It allows token teams to instantly list their tokens on the platform. Users have the option to manually remove non-compliant tokens, with the contract owner able to manage removal later. Notably, Slinky Swap converts Ethereum fees from transactions into liquidity for the Slinky Doge token through a dedicated swap and liquify function, enhancing the platform's liquidity pool.

Establishment of a DAO for Marketing Wallet Management
The marketing wallet is evolving into a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), governed by community voting. All funds will undergo community approval for usage. After launching the NFT game and marketplace, Slinky fees will fund this contract, ensuring ongoing marketing efforts with community participation and transparency.

Introduction of Geysers: Initiating Liquidity Incentives
Geysers are introduced to provide liquidity incentives within the Slinky Doge ecosystem. They encourage users to contribute liquidity by offering high APY. This mechanism helps deepen the liquidity pool, enhancing the overall efficiency and stability of the platform.
